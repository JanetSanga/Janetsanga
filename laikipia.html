HOME

Laikipia County is one of the 47 counties in Kenya. It is located on the Equator in the former Riftvalley and is widely known for tourism
and ranching. The county name means treeless plain and is a description of the county which is a vast plain with wildlife and domestic animals roaming on its rangelands.

ABOUT

It's main economic activities are crop farming, livestock rearing and tourism. The County has an area of 9,462 km2 and has a population of 518,560.
